Course overview

The course aims to equip the students with ideas and tools in statistics which range from the very beginning of the subject to an intermediate level. Together, we will examine a collection of basic concepts and commonly used methods, with an emphasis on the understanding of when and how to apply them, and why. Students will also experiment the ideas on data examples using statistical software.

Topics include (1) collection, summary and display of data, (2) estimation, hypothesis testing, and confidence statements, and (3) simple and multiple linear regression. If time permits, we will also discuss likelihood based inference.

Textbook
